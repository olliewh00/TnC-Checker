How it works

A simple scan with clear results

This tool reads the Terms & Conditions text you paste and looks for privacy-related keywords. It does not send your text to a server, and it does not use AI APIs.

1. Paste policy text

Copy any part of a Terms of Service or Privacy Policy into the input area.

2. Run the Simplify check

Click Simplify to detect risk indicators like tracking, location collection, and third-party sharing.

3. Read the report

You get a plain-language summary, a warning list, and a privacy score: Low, Medium, or High risk.
