Why this matters

Most people agree first and read later

Terms and Conditions often include critical details about data collection, tracking, and sharing practices. These details are important but hard to scan quickly.

This tool helps you understand the practical privacy impact in plain language before you click Agree.

Know what data is collected

Spot words like personal information and location so you can make a more informed choice.

Understand sharing behavior

Catch hints that your data may be shared with third parties or used beyond core service needs.

See tracking signals quickly

Tracking and cookie references can indicate behavior monitoring over time.

Reduce blind acceptance

A quick score helps you pause and decide whether the tradeoff feels acceptable to you.
